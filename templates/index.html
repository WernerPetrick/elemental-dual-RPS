Elemental Dual

How to play

Step into the enchanting realm of "Guardians of the Elements," a captivating game that immerses you in the eternal clash of the Fire, Water, and Forest Guardians. Embark on a journey of strategic choices against the computer, where each Guardian holds the power to triumph over another.

  • The Fire Guardian triumphs over the Forest Guardian
  • The Forest Guardian triumphs over the Water Guardian
  • The Water Guardian triumphs over the Fire Guardian

How to Play

  1. Select your Guardian
  2. Once your Guardian has been selected, you will be taken to the battlefield where your Guardian will oppose the computer's Guardian.
  3. The outcome of the dual will be revealed once you click on the "Fight" button
  4. To play again, click on the "Play Again" button
